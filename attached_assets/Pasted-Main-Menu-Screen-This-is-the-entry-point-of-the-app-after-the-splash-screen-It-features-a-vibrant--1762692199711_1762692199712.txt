Main Menu Screen
This is the entry point of the app after the splash screen. It features a vibrant, fun background with animated elements like floating charades cards or emoji icons to set a playful tone, inspired by games like Heads Up! and Charades!. The layout is simple and intuitive for quick navigation.

Key Elements:
Title: "Charades Fun!" (or similar, large and colorful at the top).
Buttons:
"Quick Start": Large button to jump straight into a solo or random team game.
"Create Teams": Button to set up custom teams for multiplayer.
"How to Play": Access rules and tutorials.
"Settings": For app preferences like sound, notifications, or account management.
"Subscribe": Prominent button (perhaps with a badge showing "Unlock More Categories!") leading to subscription options.

Footer: Version info, privacy policy link, and perhaps a small ad banner for free users.
Visuals: Funny icons or avatars peeking from the sides to encourage interaction.

Game Setup Screen
This screen appears after Quick Start or Team Creation. It's the final prep before gameplay, combining category and settings choices, akin to deck selection in popular charades apps.

Key Elements:
Header: "Customize Your Game" (shows team names if created).
Options:
Category Selection: Expanded grid with free categories available. Paid categories (more variety like Accents, Dance Moves, Fairy Tales) are highlighted with a "Premium" badge. Free users get 3-5; subscribers unlock 20+.
Round Time: Same as Quick Start (60s, 90s, 120s).
Advanced Settings: Toggles for activity types (e.g., Acting Only, Singing/Dancing modes from Charades! apps), difficulty (easy/hard words), and number of rounds (3-5 default).

Buttons:
"Start Game": Launches gameplay.
"Back": Return to previous screen (Quick Start or Team Creation).

Visuals: Category cards with descriptions and sample words (blurred for spoilers).


Gameplay Screen
The core playing interface, where the action happens. Modeled after the forehead-holding mechanic in Heads Up! and Charades!, with tilt controls for pass/correct.

Key Elements:
Display: Large word/phrase in bold text, with category hint at the top.
Timer: Countdown bar (e.g., 60s) with color changes (green to red as time runs low).
Controls:
Tilt down (device motion) for "Correct" (green flash, sound effect).
Tilt up for "Pass" (red flash, no penalty).

Team Indicator: Current team's name/icon/color in the corner.
Visuals: Minimalist to avoid distraction ‚Äì background matches team color, subtle animations for tilts.
End of Round: Auto-transitions to Round Results Screen when time expires.

Key Elements:
Header: "Round Over! [Team Name]'s Turn Results"
Stats:

Score: Points awarded (e.g., 1 per correct).

Buttons:

Visuals: Confetti animation for high scores, team icons celebrating.
